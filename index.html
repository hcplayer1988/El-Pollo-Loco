EL POLLO LOCO

Game instructions

Move with it A to the left and with D to the right

Jump with W

Throw salsa bottles with Space

Defeat the chickens by jumping on them or throwing salsa bottles at them!
Be careful, you only have a limited number of salsa bottles and you still need enough for the final boss!

Defeat the final boss to win by throwing salsa bottles at him!

left / right = a/d, jump = w, throw bottle = space
